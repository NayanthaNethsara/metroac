Our Services

At Metro Air, we offer a comprehensive range of air conditioning solutions to meet the needs of both homeowners and businesses.

Installation

Our team of experts will install your AC unit quickly and efficiently, ensuring that it is properly sized and positioned to provide optimal performance.

Energy-Efficiency Assessment

Our technicians can assess your AC unit and make recommendations to improve its energy efficiency, helping you save money on your monthly energy bill.

Repair

If your AC unit stops working, our technicians are available 24/7 to diagnose the problem and make the necessary repairs.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential for keeping your AC running smoothly and efficiently.
